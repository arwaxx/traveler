The best tourist destinations in Africa

Sun City

This city is one of the most wonderful cities to visit, which is located in the northwestern part of South Africa and is considered one

of the most important areas in Africa with tourist attractions.It is considered a very distinguished place for entertainment

entertainment and hospitality, and it contains a landscape like the valley of the waves and the Pilanesberg Games Center


Serengeti National Park

The Serengeti National Park in Tanzania may be the best park in the world to see wild animals.Where animals such as lions ,leopards,

giraffes, buffaloes, rhinos and deer can be seen every year, the park witnesses an event known as the Serengeti migration a migration

, involving millions of wild animals, and known gatherings of large animals more than anywhere else in the world.


Victoria Falls

Victoria Falls, which is one of the most important tourist attractions in Africa, is located in the state of Zambia on the borders of Zimbabwe

, specifically on the Zambezi River, the fourth largest river in Africa, and Victoria Falls is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in the world;

As they are the only waterfalls in the world that are more than one kilometer long and more than 100 meters high.


karnak temple

It is the largest ancient religious site and represents the joint achievement of many generations of Egyptian builders.

It actually consists of 3 temples, which are the main temple, small temples and external temples. One of the most famous

places in Karnak is the Great Hall of Columns, which is 5000 square meters. It has 134 columns arranged in 16 rows


Marrakesh

The city of Marrakesh in Morocco is one of the most beautiful cities in Africa. It is a mixture of authenticity and modernity. It is a model of Moroccan cultural life. It includes many

museums and monuments, which are among the most important tourist attractions in the country. It is also a gateway to the Atlas Mountain.
